Top PC Games of 2023

According to Marty!

Hades

Everything so polished and solid with a story adapted perfectly for the gameplay loop. 10/10

Baldur's Gate 3

Not my personal cup of tea, but an ambitious project that largely met all its production goals and did an excellent job at bringing D&D tabletop gameplay into PC gaming. 9/10

Beacon Pines

Adorable story lovingly executed to mimic old-school choose your own adventure stories. 7/10

The Medium

Not as polished as some of the others, but a great addition to the horror stealth genre with some interesting puzzles to solve. 6/10
